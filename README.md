# KisanMitra

KisanMitra is a voice-first farming assistant prototype developed during the FixForward hackathon. It helps small and medium-scale farmers make better decisions throughout the crop cycle using real-time weather updates, crop guidance, market price insights, and government scheme awareness.

## Problem Statement

Small and medium-scale farmers in India often face income loss due to the lack of timely and reliable information on weather conditions, crop risks, market prices, and financial support schemes. Climate uncertainty and limited access to actionable agricultural data make crop planning, harvesting, and selling highly unpredictable.

## Solution

KisanMitra is designed as a voice-based assistant that supports farmers in their native language.

The system helps farmers by:

* Suggesting suitable crops based on location, soil type, and weather
* Recommending the best sowing time
* Providing crop management guidance
* Offering market price insights to decide when to sell
* Recommending relevant government schemes and explaining eligibility

The solution is optimized for low-data and low-connectivity environments to ensure accessibility for rural users.

## Expected Impact

* Increase farmer net income by 10–20% per season
* Reduce losses from poor timing and distress sales by up to 20%
* Improve awareness and access to government support schemes
* Strengthen long-term rural financial resilience
